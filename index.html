About Me

I am a PhD Estudent in Systems Engineering in the Department of Computer Science. Universidad Nacional, Facultad de Minas. My research interests include Geographic Information Science (GIS), remote sensing, and Machine Learning (ML). More specifically, I am interested in applying geoespatial big data and ML to environmental modeling, specially Land use and land cover change (LULCC). I am passionate about life and knowledge, always in constant learning.
